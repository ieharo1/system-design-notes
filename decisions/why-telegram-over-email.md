# Decision: Telegram vs Email Alerts

## Problem Statement

Critical system alerts were not acted upon quickly.

---

## Comparison

| Criteria | Email | Telegram |
|------|------|------|
Latency | High | Low |
Mobile UX | Poor | Excellent |
Reliability | Medium | High |
Setup | Complex | Simple |

---

## Final Decision

Telegram was chosen due to speed, simplicity and visibility.
